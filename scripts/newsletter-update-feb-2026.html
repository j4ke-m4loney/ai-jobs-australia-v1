  1. The market is heating up fast — February is on track for a 36% increase in job post compared to January. Apply clicks have more than doubled. People aren't just browsing anymore, they're going for it.
  2. AI Engineer is the hottest title right now — it's the most posted role and the most searched. If you're positioning yourself in that space, the demand is clearly there.

  6. Entry-level interest is growing — "Junior" and "Entry Level" are increasingly popular search terms. Good news: there are 8 internships listed this month, and 97% of roles show medium-to-high promotion potential.
